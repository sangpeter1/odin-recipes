Bacon Mac and Cheese

This recipe bulks up a traditional Mac and Cheese into a healthier and yummier meal that would strect it to at least two meals. This is a perfect recipe for those who want to have an easy-to-make delicious meal on their meal prep rotation.

Ingredients

  • four strips of bacon
  • two boxes of Mac and Cheese
  • a bag of frozen peas
  • one bell peppers
  • a medium onion, chopped into tiny pieces
  • one clove of garlic, minced
  • a batch of spinach
Steps
  1. In a small pan, fry bacon until they become crispy. Set them aside on a plate
  2. In a medium saucepan, boil water. As soon as the water starts to boil, put the pasta. Cook for 7-9 minutes. Drain the pasta and put it back in the suacepan.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, fry bell peppers on the same pan used for bacon earlier. Fry it for about two minutes and then add chopped onion. When the onions become translucent, add the minced garlic. Fry it until the raw smell of garlic disappears. Add the frozen peas stir until the peas thaw. Add spinach at the end and stir until it wilts.
  4. Mix the pasta and with the powdered chesse it comes with. Then mix it all with the cooked vegetables.
  5. OPTIONAL: Sprinkle shredded parmesan if you want to make it cheesier.
